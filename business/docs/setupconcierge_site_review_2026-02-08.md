## Observations & Recommendations

### Strengths

1. **Professional polish** — the site looks premium and trustworthy
2. **Clear value proposition** — "Don't Let a Bad Setup Cost You Your Brand" is direct
3. **Structured services** — numbered steps create a clear flow
4. **Intake process** — Client Intake Request Form shows readiness

### Opportunities

1. **WP Engine badge** — appears slightly pixelated; consider swapping for a higher-res version
2. **Social proof** — testimonials or case studies would strengthen credibility (WNQ could be first)
3. **Pricing transparency** — consider adding starting prices or package tiers
4. **Blog/Insights** — regular content would boost SEO and authority
5. **Portfolio section** — showcase completed client work (WNQ brand revamp, photo sessions)
